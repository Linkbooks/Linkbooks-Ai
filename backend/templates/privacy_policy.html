Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 29/11/2024

Linkbooks Ltd ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our services, including the Linkbooks Ai App ("the Service").

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

We may collect personal information you provide directly, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business details (e.g., company name, address)

2. Technical Data

We may automatically collect technical data when you use our services, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version

  • Usage statistics (e.g., API calls, log data)

3. Third-Party Integrations

When you connect your QuickBooks account to our Service, we may access the following data with your consent:

  • Company information
  • Financial reports (e.g., Profit & Loss, Balance Sheets)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Facilitate AI-based business data analysis.
  • Fulfill legal or contractual obligations.
  • Respond to customer service inquiries or technical support requests.
  • Communicate updates, security alerts, or policy changes.

Data Sharing and Security

1. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share your data:

  • With trusted third-party services (e.g., OpenAI, Supabase) to operate the Service.
  • When required to comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

2. Data Security

We implement robust security measures, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest.
  • Regular vulnerability assessments.
  • Restricted access to personal data.

However, no online system is entirely secure. Users are advised to take precautions when sharing sensitive data.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws, such as:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data.
  • Correction: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format.
  • Objection/Restriction: Object to or restrict certain data processing.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@wawstudios.com.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance user experience.
  • Track service performance.
  • Support authentication.

Manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner.

International Data Transfers

For users in the UK or EU, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction, including the United States. We ensure adequate safeguards, such as standard contractual clauses, are in place.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The effective date will be updated, and changes will be highlighted prominently on our website.
